Questions
Everything you need
to know
What exactly does the agent do? Is this just a chatbot? ▼
No — it's not a chatbot. Our agents perform real, multi-step tasks autonomously: reading emails, drafting replies, researching topics across the web, scheduling meetings, and pushing data to your tools. Think of it as a junior employee who works 24/7 and never makes the same mistake twice.
How does the agent learn my business? ▼
During setup you provide: your company description, past email examples, your docs/FAQs, and escalation rules. The agent uses this as its knowledge base and gets smarter every week based on your feedback and approvals.
What happens if the agent makes a mistake? ▼
By default, agents are set to "draft mode" for the first 7 days — meaning they prepare replies but don't send until you approve. Once you're confident, you can enable autonomous mode. High-stakes actions (refunds, account changes) always require human approval regardless of mode.
What integrations are supported? ▼
Gmail, Outlook, Slack, Notion, Google Docs, Airtable, HubSpot, and webhooks. Stripe and Intercom integrations are in beta. Custom integrations are available on the Enterprise plan.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes, absolutely. Month-to-month billing. Cancel before your renewal date and you won't be charged again. We don't do contracts, lock-ins, or cancellation fees. Ever.
Is my data secure? ▼
Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never use your data to train models. Enterprise customers get a dedicated agent instance — no data is shared between customers. We can sign NDAs on request.
What's your refund policy? ▼
If your agent isn't live within 24 hours of payment, you get a full refund, no questions asked. For general dissatisfaction in the first 7 days, email okfreelancer@proton.me and we'll make it right — refund or credit at your choice.
